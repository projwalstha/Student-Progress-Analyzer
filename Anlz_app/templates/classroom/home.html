This system is a unique invention that can track the progress of students using computerized online MCQ tests Teachers can regularly conduct MCQ tests according to the subject chapters and know the understanding of the students in the subject matters and can help him in the subject matters.

Computerized tests can provide various benefits to the manual tests as it can requires less time to conduct and can generate results quickly without needing any external manpower. The system will be able to provide instant results of the exam with their grades.
